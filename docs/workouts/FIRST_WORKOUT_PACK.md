# FIRST_WORKOUT_PACK.md — The Corner Foundations Collection

The first official Corner workout collection: eight authored sessions that demonstrate the framework and give athletes a real curriculum from day one. Each is written to this standard (`AUTHORING_GUIDE.md`, `WORKOUT_SCHEMA.md`, `ROUND_LIBRARY.md`, `CUE_LIBRARY.md`) and passes `QUALITY_CHECKLIST.md`.

Together they span stances, all six workout types, and the full difficulty range — proving the framework and forming a coherent starting path. Round timing shown as `work / rest`. Representative cues are illustrative, not the full script.

**Collection arc (a suggested path):** Footwork Essentials → Orthodox Power (or Southpaw Foundations) → Defensive Movement → Counter Punching → Pressure Fighting → Fight Conditioning, with Recovery Session woven in on light days.

---

## 1. Footwork Essentials
- **Purpose:** build the engine of boxing — balance, range, and angles — before power.
- **Target athlete:** beginner–intermediate; orthodox or southpaw. The right *first* Corner workout.
- **Difficulty:** Beginner · **Type:** technical · **Duration:** ~16 min · **Timing:** 4×(120/60)
- **Skills taught:** staying on the balls of the feet, keeping stance, cutting angles, jab-and-move.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — light movement, find range. "Stay light. Find your distance."
  2. *Footwork* — move and hold stance. "Off the balls of your feet." · "Don't cross the feet."
  3. *Jab and Move* — marry the jab to the feet. "Jab — and step." · "Hit and don't be there."
  4. *Footwork (angles)* — cut angles off the jab. "Cut the angle." · "Own the outside."
  5. *Cooldown* — slow movement, reinforce balance. Honest close.
- **Coach packs:** Technical (default), Calm.

## 2. Orthodox Power
- **Purpose:** build power and precision from orthodox stance — straight punches, from the ground up.
- **Target athlete:** intermediate orthodox boxer with solid fundamentals.
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate · **Type:** technical → combinations · **Duration:** ~20 min · **Timing:** 4×(180/60)
- **Skills taught:** the cross with hip rotation, jab-cross timing, adding the lead hook, power from the legs.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — range-finding jabs, loosen the hips.
  2. *Straight Right* — the power hand. "Turn the hip." · "Sit down on the cross." · "Hands back."
  3. *Double Jab → Cross* — set up the power. "One-two — snap the jab, drive the cross."
  4. *Lead Hook added* — jab, cross, hook. "Tight on the hook — turn, don't swing." · "Guard on the open side."
  5. *Cooldown* — shadow the combination slow, reinforce mechanics. Honest close.
- **Coach packs:** Old School (default), Technical, Competition.

## 3. Southpaw Foundations
- **Purpose:** coach the southpaw's actual game — the straight left, the outside foot, and angles vs. orthodox. Not an orthodox workout mirrored.
- **Target athlete:** beginner–intermediate southpaw (badly served by every other app).
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate · **Type:** technical · **Duration:** ~18 min · **Timing:** 4×(150/60) · **Stance:** southpaw
- **Skills taught:** the straight left down the middle, winning the outside-foot position, the lead-hand game, stepping to angles.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — find southpaw range, feel the lead-foot battle.
  2. *Southpaw Specific (straight left)* — "Straight left on the line." · "Down the middle." · "Hand back."
  3. *Southpaw Specific (outside foot)* — "Step outside the lead foot." · "Own the angle."
  4. *Angle attack* — straight left off the step. "Step to the angle — then the left." · "Make him turn."
  5. *Cooldown* — groove the straight left slow; identity-affirming close ("that's the southpaw edge — use it").
- **Coach packs:** Southpaw (default and strongly recommended), Technical.

## 4. Defensive Movement
- **Purpose:** learn not to get hit — slips, rolls, guard, and the reset — as a foundation for countering.
- **Target athlete:** intermediate; orthodox or southpaw.
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate · **Type:** defense · **Duration:** ~20 min · **Timing:** 4×(180/60)
- **Skills taught:** slipping small and balanced, rolling under, keeping the guard home, resetting after defending.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — gentle head movement, loosen the neck and hips.
  2. *Defence (slip)* — slip the jab, stay balanced. "Slip small — stay on your line." · "Hands home."
  3. *Defence (roll)* — roll under the hook. "Roll under — chin down." · "Don't drop the hands to move."
  4. *Defence (reset)* — defend, then reset to punch. "Defend — and reset." · "Never freeze after you slip."
  5. *Cooldown* — slow slips and rolls; reinforce staying balanced. Honest close.
- **Coach packs:** Technical (default), Calm.

## 5. Counter Punching
- **Purpose:** turn defense into offense — slip and answer instantly.
- **Target athlete:** intermediate–advanced with sound defense (prerequisite: Defensive Movement).
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate → Advanced · **Type:** defense → combinations · **Duration:** ~22 min · **Timing:** 5×(180/60)
- **Skills taught:** slip-and-cross, roll-and-hook, countering off the guard, the instant answer.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — head movement + sharp jabs.
  2. *Single Jab* — re-establish range and the lead hand.
  3. *Counter Punching (slip → cross)* — "Slip the jab — fire the cross." · "The answer follows instantly."
  4. *Counter Punching (roll → hook)* — "Roll under — come back with the hook."
  5. *Fight Pace (apply)* — counters at pace, into combinations. "Make him miss — make him pay." · "Compose, then fire."
  6. *Cooldown* — shadow the counters slow. Honest close.
- **Coach packs:** Technical (default), Fight Night, Competition.

## 6. Pressure Fighting
- **Purpose:** learn to walk an opponent down — cut the ring, stay behind the jab, relentless but controlled.
- **Target athlete:** advanced; orthodox or southpaw.
- **Difficulty:** Advanced · **Type:** fight-simulation → conditioning · **Duration:** ~22 min · **Timing:** 5×(180/45)
- **Skills taught:** cutting the ring, pressing behind punches, guard while advancing, body work inside.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — active movement, sharp jabs, raise the pace early.
  2. *Pressure (behind the jab)* — "Step in behind the jab." · "Cut the ring — don't chase straight."
  3. *Pressure (to the body)* — "Dig to the body." · "Hands up while you press."
  4. *Fight Pace* — bursts and resets pressing forward. "Flurry — then reset." · "Stay composed at pace."
  5. *Fight Simulation* — put it together, dramatic finish. "This is the round — walk him down." → countdown.
  6. *Cooldown* — bring it all the way down. Honest close.
- **Coach packs:** Fight Night (default), Competition, Old School.

## 7. Fight Conditioning
- **Purpose:** build work capacity and fight-pace endurance — output and form under fatigue.
- **Target athlete:** intermediate–advanced looking to build the tank.
- **Difficulty:** Advanced · **Type:** conditioning · **Duration:** ~24 min · **Timing:** 6×(180/45)
- **Skills taught:** sustained output, pace variation, breathing under load, holding guard when tired.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — build the heart rate deliberately.
  2. *Conditioning* — steady high output. "Keep the output up." · "Breathe with the shots."
  3. *Fight Pace* — flurries and resets. "Burst — then breathe." · "Don't gas — pace it."
  4. *Conditioning (body)* — sustained body work. "Dig — stay low, stay busy."
  5. *Fight Pace* — the hard one. "Hold the guard when you're tired — that's the round that counts."
  6. *Fight Simulation* — empty the tank, big finish. "Last round — everything you've got." → countdown.
  7. *Cooldown* — long, slow recovery. Honest close acknowledging the grind.
- **Coach packs:** Competition (default), Fight Night, Old School.

## 8. Recovery Session
- **Purpose:** active recovery and technique flow — sustainable training on light days.
- **Target athlete:** any level; a rest-day or between-hard-sessions workout.
- **Difficulty:** Beginner (low intensity, any level) · **Type:** recovery · **Duration:** ~16 min · **Timing:** 4×(150/75)
- **Skills taught:** staying loose, breathing, smooth mechanics without strain, grooving fundamentals.
- **Round breakdown:**
  1. *Warmup* — very light movement, deep breaths.
  2. *Recovery (jab flow)* — easy, clean jabs. "Long, easy breaths." · "Smooth and sharp — no strain."
  3. *Recovery (movement)* — light footwork and rolls. "Stay loose — just flow."
  4. *Recovery (shadow flow)* — string easy combinations gently. "Groove it — soft and clean."
  5. *Cooldown* — breathing and stillness. Warm, calm close ("light days are training days — well done").
- **Coach packs:** Calm (default and strongly recommended).

## 9. Collection notes

- **Coverage:** all six workout types (`technical, combinations, defense, conditioning, fight-simulation, recovery`), both stances, and Beginner → Advanced are represented — a complete first curriculum.
- **Relationships:** `prerequisites` and `progressionNext` link these into a path (e.g. Defensive Movement → Counter Punching; Footwork Essentials as the entry point; Recovery Session available throughout).
- **Every workout** declares its objective, arc, coach-pack fit, and passes the read-aloud test — proving the framework produces sessions that feel unmistakably like Corner.
- **This pack is the reference standard.** New authors should study these eight before writing their own — match their quality, pacing, language, and philosophy, and any future pack will belong in Corner.
